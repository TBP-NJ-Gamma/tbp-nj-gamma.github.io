About Us

Tau Beta Pi

Tau Beta Pi is the oldest national engineering honor society in the United States, established in 1885. The society is dedicated to promoting academic excellence, professionalism, character, and community service. The NJ Gamma chapter proudly represents New Jersey Institute of Technology, cultivating a supportive environment that encourages networking, leadership development, and community engagement.

Networking

Being part of TBP means involved in an ecosystem with professionals and peers alike. Events held on campus give students the opportunity to reach out to fellow TBP members, make connections, and grow a network of admired engineers. Growing this network not only opens up doors for professional development, but also allows members to make new friends, broaden their academic interests, or see the world from a different lens.
